Moving on to CSS, I skillfully apply styling properties to elevate the visual presentation. With attention to detail and a keen eye for design, I bring the website to life, ensuring a seamless and captivating experience for visitors. Through my expertise in HTML and CSS, I strive to create websites that not only meet functional requirements but also leave a lasting impression on users.
